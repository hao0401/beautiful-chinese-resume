# Targeting Keywords

## Target Company Use

Use target company for:

- Choosing the most relevant experiences.
- Ordering sections and bullets.
- Selecting natural keywords.
- Naming the output file.
- Adjusting tone for industry and role.

Do not usually print the target company in the resume body. The target role is more appropriate at the top.

## JD Matching

Extract nouns and verbs from the JD, then map them to provided facts. Prefer natural wording over keyword stuffing. A good final resume should contain the strongest relevant terms two or three times across headings, bullets, and skills, not every possible keyword.
